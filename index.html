Exciting classes for adventurous people

You're going to fall in love with English

I have spent a few years, full time in a live classroom as well as online teaching, helping students from many backgrounds, such as business professionals, doctors and IELTS students master the English language and gain confidence in expressing their thoughts in English.

Explore English like you never have before

The strength of my programme is that I will be drawing a lot from my past experience of what has successfully worked in a simplified style.

Each lesson steeped in everyday practical set-up scenarios about food and drinks at restaurants or perhaps drafting emails, telephoning collegues and bosses plus meeting room conversations in the office environment. By the end of each lesson, you will be able to converse in the given topic with fluently.

Professionally or for everyday social English style.

If you allow me I will help time to practice speaking as I guide the conversation through signs and symbols so you practice grammar and vocabulary through each exercise. Every moment you open your mouth you will have improved already. I will also help you gain an English ear through guided listening exercises where I will guide you before we start each listening exercise on how you should listen for each exercise. At the end of each lesson, the expectation is that you can articulate your thoughts clearly and accurately.
